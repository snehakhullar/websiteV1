Wireframing


Design is a plan for arranging elements in such a way as best to accomplish a particular purpose.

Charles Eames

I use wireframes to: (1) present and brainstorm design iterations based on feedback from various stakeholders, in a quick and efficient manner, (2) perform usability testing, (3) provide starting point for visual designers to create high fidelity mock-ups, and (4) aid developers in implementing the product functionality

Survey 123

Time frame:Jan - Feb 2015

Role:Interaction designer

Webpage link:Click here

Survey 123 is a product for creating, managing, distributing surveys and viewing survey results spatially.

Objective

Enable agile product development

What I did

  • Worked with product owners to refine product requirements, conceptualized design and developed wireframes using Balsamiq Mock-ups
  • Created weekly design iterations of low-fidelity mock-ups
  • Coordinated with developers to create front-end frameworks and with visual designers to create visual assets

Impact

  • Released beta product within 4 weeks, at an Esri Federal conference
